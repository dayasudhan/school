ABOUT KHAANAVALI

"Khaanavali" is the North Karnataka word for a Hotel. It is a famous hotspot for the kannadigas wherein various spicy dishes of North Karnataka are being served and relished.

Our "Khaanavali" is a Food Ordering and Delivery company based out of Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. The main purpose is to provide a complete food ordering and delivery solution from the best khaanavalis across the city to those people who like to relish their native styled cuisines. Thus, encouraging our vendors through our customers to grow their ventures thereby. Our Policies, Payments and delivery system are simple and exclusive. We ensure On-time Deliveries from the vendor's personnel.

ABOUT US

Firstly, we are Proud Kannadigas ! Having years of professional experience in the software industry across top MNC's, the team of Khaanavali came up with an idea to promote and encourage fellow kannadigas to grow their businesses. Hence, the "KHAANAVALI.com".
