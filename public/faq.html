Frequently Asked Questions

1.) I'm facing an Issue with the Website/App !

Please write to us at khaanavali@gmail.com with all the details.

2.) Can I place an Order over a Phone ?

All orders are to be placed through the Website/App only.

3.) Will you be Responsible for the Quality of the Food ?

We only collect food on your behalf from the outlets and deliver it. And we won’t be accountable for the quality of the food. In case you have any Complaint/Feedback, please write in through the Contact Us webpage and we’ll take it up with the Restaurant in question. Don't forget to tell us the Order Number or Restaurant Name.

4.) How do I contact Khaanavali customer care ?

You can write to us at khaanavali@gmail.com until customer care service has been setup. We'll notify through the message about the availibility of the said service.

5.) I am a Restaurant 0wner, How do I tie-up with Khaanavali ?

You can write to us at khaanavali@gmail.com with the Details of your Restaurant and Contact person's Name, Mobile and Email-Id. Our sales team will get in touch with you post reviewing the same.

6.) What are your delivery hours ?

Our Delivery Hours are subjected to the rules of your city and the Timings of each Restaurant.

7.) How much time do you take to Deliver ?

This is usually between 30 to 45 minutes ( depends on the traffic also ). We will mention estimated time on Details of every Restaurant.

8.) Can I order from Multiple Restaurant in a Single Order ?

No. However, you can place orders for Individual Items from different Restaurants as we do not impose a Minimum Order Value.

9.) Can I Order in Advance ?

No, you cannot order in Advance. All our orders are placed and executed On-Demand.

10.) Which Cities are you Currently serving ?

Currently we are serving Bangalore.
